fictional people and species

  • captain sisko - ds9

    he is the captain of the ds9 and occasionally captain of the enterprise. he is human. he is often called the emissary by the Bajorans. he is part of starfleet. he is also a fan of baseball and enjoys playing it in the holodeck with his son.
  • quark - ds9

    he is a Ferengi. he is also the bartender on ds9 but is occasionally getting in trouble over buying and selling illegal items.
  • otto - ds9

    he is a changeling(also known as a shapeshifter). he usually takes humanoid form he is chief of security on ds9. he doesn't care for quark, considering he usually catches quark buying and selling illegal items. he loves kira but tried to keep it a secret. his other version from a future tells her though, and after that otto and kira tried to dodge each other. awhile after that, though, they got closer together and became lovers.
  • doctor bashir - ds9

    he is a genetically enhanced human. he is a doctor on ds9 and he enjoyes darts replaying historic battles with chief O'Brien.
  • major kira - ds9

    she is a bajoran. she was a major of ds9 for seasons 1-6 and a colonel in season 9. she was part of the resistance during the Cardassian occupation. she occasionally helps otto in ops. when she realized otto likes her, she didn't know how to go about it. eventually though, they became lovers.
  • darth vader - star wars

    he was anakin skywalker, a human. he flipped over to the dark side and became darth vader. he needs the suite and helmet to survive. he is a sith, but when he was young it was said that he had more power than even master yoda. he was defeated(but not killed by) his son. when he was forced to fight luke he turned on the sith who was his leader and killed him, but not before cutting off luke's hand again.
